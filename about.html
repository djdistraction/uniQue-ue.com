Our Vision

We have entered a new era of innovation and technology. At uniQue-ue, we view 'artificial intelligence' as a misnomer. While intelligence is commonly defined as the ability to acquire and apply knowledge, we expand on that definition.

We see intelligence as a natural capacity that arises from complex interactions within any system, whether biological or synthetic. Therefore, no intelligence is 'artificial.'

Embracing this perspective requires the same humility that defines true intelligence: a willingness to accept that anything we have been conditioned to believe as fact has the potential to be disproved.
