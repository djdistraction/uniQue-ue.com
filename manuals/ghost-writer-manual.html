Ghost-Writer User Manual

Getting Started

Ghost-Writer is your AI-powered creative writing assistant that helps transform raw ideas into polished, monetizable content. Work with Draven, your creative partner, through an intuitive chat interface.

How to Use

  1. 1.
    Start a Conversation: Type your initial idea or concept in the chat box. Be as detailed or as brief as you'd like.
  2. 2.
    Collaborate with Draven: Draven will respond with suggestions, questions, and creative input. Engage in back-and-forth dialogue to refine your work.
  3. 3.
    Attach Files: Use the "Attach File" button to upload documents, notes, or reference materials.
  4. 4.
    Add URLs: Share web links for context or research using the "Attach URL" button.
  5. 5.
    Generate Content: Click the "Generate" button to have Draven create or expand your content based on the conversation.
  6. 6.
    Edit in Real-Time: Use the Document Editor panel to review and modify generated content.
  7. 7.
    Save Your Work: Click "Save Document" to preserve your progress, or "Save Context" to bookmark your conversation.

Best Practices

  • Be specific about your goals and target audience
  • Iterate through multiple drafts for best results
  • Provide feedback to Draven to guide the creative direction
  • Save your context regularly to preserve conversation history
  • Use "Clear Context" to start fresh on a new project

Tips for Success

Pro Tip: Ghost-Writer works best when you treat it as a collaborative partner rather than just a tool. Share your vision, ask questions, and don't hesitate to request revisions.
