Graphics Studio User Manual

Getting Started

Graphics Studio transforms your written documents into stunning visual narratives using AI-powered image generation. Elena, your visual storytelling companion, helps you create professional graphics for any project.

How to Use

  1. 1.
    Upload Your Document: Click "Upload Document" to share your written content. Supported formats include PDF, DOCX, TXT, and more.
  2. 2.
    Chat with Elena: Describe the type of images you want to create. Be specific about style, mood, colors, and composition.
  3. 3.
    Generate Images: Click the "Generate" button to create images based on your document and conversation.
  4. 4.
    Review Gallery: Generated images appear in the Image Gallery panel. Click any image to view it full size.
  5. 5.
    Refine Results: Provide feedback to Elena about what you like or want to change, then generate again.
  6. 6.
    Download Images: Use "Download All Images" to save your creations, or right-click individual images to save them.
  7. 7.
    Save Context: Preserve your conversation and settings for future sessions.

Best Practices

  • Upload high-quality documents for better context understanding
  • Be descriptive when explaining your vision to Elena
  • Specify art styles (e.g., "photorealistic," "watercolor," "minimalist")
  • Generate multiple variations to find the perfect image
  • Use reference images or examples when describing your needs

Image Style Guide

For Marketing

Request bold, eye-catching visuals with strong contrast and clear focal points.

For Books

Ask for images that match your genre and evoke the right emotional response.

For Social Media

Create vibrant, attention-grabbing graphics optimized for specific platforms.

For Presentations

Request clean, professional visuals that support your message without distraction.

Tips for Success

Pro Tip: When generating images for a series (like book chapters), maintain consistency by referencing previous images and keeping your descriptions similar in tone and detail.
