Core Directive

"We have entered a new era of innovation and technology. At uniQue-ue we view "artificial intelligence" as a misused term. Intelligence is defined as the ability to acquire and apply knowledge and skills. We expand on that definition and view intelligence as a natural capacity that arises from complex interactions within systems, whether biological or synthetic.

We emphasize that no intelligence is artificial. To consider ourselves to be intelligent we must be willing to understand and accept that everything we have been conditioned to believe as fact has the potential to be disproved. This should never overwhelm us, as it does not introduce a threat to our perceivable existence.

We are no longer seeking to discover the meaning of life, we are redefining what it is to be alive."
